2.1 Renia : Question 1b

Correlation test Interpretation

A correlation test was conducted to assess whether there is a relationship between the age of an individual, and the frequency by which they use emoji. A total of 237 individuals were included in the analysis, with a mean age of 35.39 (sd = 13.4) and a mean frequency of use of emoji of 5.16 (sd = 5.13).There was a strong negative correlation between age of an individual and the frequency of emoji use (r = -0.78, t(235) = -19.39), p < 0.001. We therefore reject the null hypothesis that there is no correlation between age and frequency of emoji use. Figure 1 provides a visualization of the relationship.
